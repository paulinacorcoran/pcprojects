To that point, I had never lived away from home or experienced truly being on my own. I wanted to pave my own way and start something for myself in a new city, and there truly is nothing like Miami. This city is not only beautiful, but features a central location to every sory of industry imaginable. The culture is extremely diverse and I have thouroughly enjoyed getting to know people from all geographical and socioeconomic backgrounds. There is also so much opportunity within the school to enrich yourself in clubs, social groups, and extracurricular activities.
